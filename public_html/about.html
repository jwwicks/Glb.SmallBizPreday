About SMB Preday

SMB Preday is a free community resource developed and maintained by SMB Books and Great Little Book Publishing Co., Inc. These are the web sites I use to promote books and other resources for the SMB Conulting community.

What's a "Preday?"

Many years ago, Harry Brelsford rented a hotel room at the site of the Microsoft Worldwide Partner Conference. He put on an event just for SMB Consultants. A whole day of activities. Because it was as close as possible to the WPC events, he attracted a lot of people who were in town for the WPC.

And thus the "preday" event was born.

In fall of 2005, I help my first preday event on the site of SMB Nation. We had a room set for 115. People were packed in that room and hanging out in the hallway. Not sure how many people we had total. Every year since then I've held a preday event for SMB Nation, and got at least 120 attendees.

Now, in 2008, there are lots of people doing preday events for a variety of conferences. I've scheduled events with with Erick Simpson, Matt Makowicz, Robin Robins, Amy Luby, and others. These are great educational opportunities -- and a lot of fun!

Plan to attend a conference. Look me up when you get there!

-- karlp
